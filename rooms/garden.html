Garden View Suite

The Garden View Suite looks out over a quiet garden area with plants and trees.

The Garden View Suite feels like a quiet retreat surrounded by greenery. Soft sky-blue and sea green accents create a calm space that looks out over a peaceful garden courtyard.

Room details:

  • Queen size bed with soft, cozy bedding
  • Large window overlooking the garden
  • Comfortable reading chair and side table
  • Garden style soaking tub with candles
  • Coastal artwork featuring shells and sea life
  • Smart TV and complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Mini fridge with chilled water and snacks

Nightly Rates

  • Weeknights (Sun–Thu): $180 per night
  • Weekends (Fri–Sat): $210 per night
  • Extended stay (3+ nights): 10% discount

This room is great for solo travelers, students on a quiet study retreat, or guests who love plants and a peaceful, cozy atmosphere.
